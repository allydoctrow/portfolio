ABOUT ME

I have always been fascinated with the feeling of being uncomfortable. My main goal with my artwork is to make people feel something—hopefully something they have not felt before. Whether it is extreme curiosity, confusion, anxiety, shame, fear, or a mix of all of these together. An element that has always played a part in my art and in my life is chaos: too many things happening at once, but they work somehow to make the piece stronger.

In my design work, I try to implement what I believe makes something dynamic. I think my ability to infuse my design background with my fine art background lends itself to design work that is creative and unlike other designers in the field.

In my fine artwork, I am primarily drawn to printmaking and drawing. I feel like these mediums speak to me because of the ability it gives me to include such fine black and white detail. I particularly enjoy relief printing and intaglio etchings.
